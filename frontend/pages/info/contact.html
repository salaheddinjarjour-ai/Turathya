Consignment Inquiries

If you are interested in consigning items for auction, please submit photos and any available documentation (provenance, past appraisals, receipts) to the appropriate specialist department.

Our specialists will review your submission and respond with a preliminary assessment. If your items are suitable for auction, we will discuss terms, timing, and reserve requirements.

Office Location

Turathya operates primarily online. Viewing of select lots can be arranged by appointment. Please contact us to schedule a visit.

Response Times

We aim to respond to all inquiries within two business days. During active auction periods, response times for bidding support may be faster. Complex consignment evaluations may require additional time for research and consultation.
