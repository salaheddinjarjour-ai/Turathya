About Turathya

Trusted platform for timed auctions of antiques and rare items

Our Mission

Turathya connects discerning collectors with exceptional pieces through carefully curated timed auctions. We specialize in antiques, fine art, rare books, ceramics, and collectibles of historical and aesthetic significance.

Our platform combines the ease of online bidding with the rigor and expertise of traditional auction houses. Every item offered is evaluated by specialists and presented with detailed condition reports and provenance documentation.

Our Approach

We believe quality trumps quantity. Rather than flooding bidders with endless listings, we focus on thoughtful curation. Each auction consists of items selected for their authenticity, condition, and cultural value.

Timed auctions allow participants from around the world to bid at their convenience, while maintaining the competitive dynamics and transparency of live sales. Bidding periods are clearly defined, increments are standardized, and all activity is recorded.

Trust and Transparency

Every piece is accompanied by comprehensive documentation including high-resolution images, detailed descriptions, condition assessments, and available provenance. We disclose all known restorations, repairs, and flaws.

Buyer's premium and fee structures are clearly outlined. Payment terms, shipping arrangements, and return policies are established before bidding begins. We operate with the same standards of accountability expected from established auction houses.

Expertise

Our team includes specialists in European decorative arts, Asian ceramics, rare books and manuscripts, fine art, and antique jewelry. Items are evaluated for authenticity, condition, and market value before being accepted into our auctions.

We work with independent experts, restorers, and appraisers when specialized knowledge is required. Third-party authentication and appraisals are obtained for high-value items or uncertain attributions.

For Collectors

Whether you are building a collection, looking for a specific piece, or exploring a new area of interest, Turathya provides access to vetted items with clear documentation. Our platform is designed for serious collectors who value expertise, transparency, and integrity.
